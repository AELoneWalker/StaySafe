HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID 19

~ made by ProGrad JR, Ashutosh

Wash your hands regularly

Regular hand wash with soap or sanitizer for
30 seconds will help prevent germs.

Cover your mouth and nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing.
It is better if you use a mask.

Stay home Stay safe

Staying at home will keep you safe
from the outside environment.

Eat Healthy

Always eating healty food will protect you
from harmful diseases.

Keep Calm

Regular exercise will keep you
healthy and strong.

BE AWARE, BE SAFE
